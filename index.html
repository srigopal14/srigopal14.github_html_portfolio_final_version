About Me

Hello! I'm Sriya Gopalan, a passionate 3rd-year Graphic Design student at San Jose State University. My interest in art started at the age of five, and I have continued to explore various creative forms throughout my life.

As a visual learner, art became a medium to enhance comprehension and expression. In my free time, I participate in school art clubs and volunteer for community programs. I look forward to collaborating on children's books that inspire creativity and promote a healthy, stress-free life.
