About Me

Hello! I'm Sriya Gopalan, a passionate 3rd-year Graphic Design student at San Jose State University. My interest in art started when I was 5 years old and I have continued to explore various creative forms of art throughout my life. As a visual learner, art was a medium for myself to help improve comprehension. During my free time, I participate in art clubs during school and volunteer for community programs. I'm looking forward to collaborating on children's books by inspiring kids for a healthy, stress-free life.
