Project Overview: Data Bending with TextEdit

Project Overview

This project is the first in an experimental digital art series, focusing on glitch art created by manually manipulating a file's code, also called data bending.

Design & Execution

  • Used TextEdit to manipulate JPEG files directly.
  • Copied and pasted sections after ~20% to avoid corrupting the header.
  • Observed glitch effects in Preview and refined the result.

Reflections

This project explores the intersection of digital errors and creativity. Each image produced unexpected visual patterns, highlighting the beauty of controlled chaos in data manipulation.
