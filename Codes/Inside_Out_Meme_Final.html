The Inside Scoop

Description

This meme explores the trend of using ChatGPT or AI for completing assignments in school. It's based on a popular Inside Out Characters meme: "Do you ever look at someone and wonder what is going on inside their head?".

The scene shows Riley's mother worrying about her daughter's anxiety, while inside Riley's mind, she contemplates using ChatGPT to avoid research by writing a paper in a 4000-word format.

Other characters inside Riley's mind (Joy, Sadness, Anger, Disgust) are hesitant about the idea, but Anxiety ultimately allows the decision to proceed with a "Good Decision" nod.
