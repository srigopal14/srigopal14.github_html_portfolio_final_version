2 Axis Music Logos: Creative Typography & Color

Project Overview

I designed a music logo for a brand called "Axis Music". The concept integrates music notes into letterforms, creating a playful and unique brand identity that combines musical structure with visual creativity.

Design & Execution

Conceptual Design: Merging music notes with letters to create a memorable brand mark.
Typography: Clean, professional font for the main logo, cursive style for the "music" element for artistic emphasis.
Color Palette: Vibrant, playful colors enhance energy and visual impact.
Tools Used: Adobe Illustrator for scalable, high-quality graphics.
