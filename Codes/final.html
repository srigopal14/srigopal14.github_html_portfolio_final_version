Thought Process

  • For my final project, I created a mashup of three artistic mediums learned in class: 3D modeling in Blender, glitch art using Photoshop, and code-based art with p5.js. I centered the project around the theme of soccer, inspired by the excitement surrounding the FIFA World Cup at Levi’s Stadium in 2026.
  • The video begins with a 3D soccer animation showing a goal scored in slow motion, emphasizing movement, lighting, and realistic shadows. I then added an animated “Gooal” text using Adobe After Effects, followed by glitch effects applied to both the text and the cheering crowd to heighten the emotional impact.
  • Based on feedback, I included a second 3D goal from an opposing perspective. I also introduced p5.js artwork depicting fans cheering, using animated shapes to represent body movement and excitement.
  • Finally, I combined all elements—Blender renders, glitch imagery, After Effects text, and p5.js animation—into a 25-second video edited in iMovie. The concluding title, “Priceless Moments,” reflects the emotional connection between the game and its fans.
